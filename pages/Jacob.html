Medicine in Computing

Computers have enabled surgery to become more advanced.
The benefits include:

  • Magnified vision
  • More accuracy from stabilised instruments
  • Improved access to the body
  • Maintained accuracy over long periods
  • Allows for remote surgery

Computing systems also allow for electronic health records.
Some advantages of this are:

  • Providing accurate and up to date information quickly
  • Sharing information across any and all medical centres
  • Encourages complete and precise documentation of patients
